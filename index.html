Cumin Potatoes

    Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes (boiled and diced)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish, optional)

Process

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add the diced boiled potatoes, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt.
  3. Stir well and cook for 5-7 minutes until the potatoes are golden and slightly crispy.
  4. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves (optional) and serve hot with roti or paratha

Scrambled Paneer

    Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (crumbled)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 2 green chilies (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish, optional)

Process

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and green chilies (if using). Cook until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Add turmeric powder, salt, and crumbled paneer. Mix well.
  5. Cook for 3-4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Garnish with coriander (optional) and serve with bread, roti, or paratha.

Lemon Rice

    Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (cooled)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tbsp peanuts
  • 1 green chili (slit)
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish, optional)

Process

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add peanuts and green chili, sauté until peanuts turn golden brown.
  3. Add turmeric powder and stir for a few seconds.
  4. Add the cooked rice, salt, and lemon juice. Mix well until the rice is coated with the turmeric and lemon juice.
  5. CCook for 2-3 minutes, garnish with coriander if desired, and serve hot.
